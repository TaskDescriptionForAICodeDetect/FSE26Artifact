You are trying to cut down a giant tree using N types of tools. Initially, the tree has D durability and your experience points are 0. When you strike the tree with tool i, the tree's durability decreases by ai and you gain ei experience points. However, you must have at least ri experience points to use tool i. The tree falls when its durability reaches 0 or below. Given the durability of the tree and the information about the tools, write a program to determine the minimum number of strikes required to cut down the tree.
