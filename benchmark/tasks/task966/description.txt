The currency used in Takahashi Kingdom is Myon. There are 1-, 10-, 100-, 1000-, and 10000-Myon coins, and so forth. Formally, there are 10^n-Myon coins for any non-negative integer n. There are N items being sold at Ex Store. The price of the i-th item is A_i Myon. Takahashi wants to buy at least one, possibly all, of these items without receiving change. He wants to pay the total price with the minimum number of coins possible. Find the minimum number of coins he must bring to the store, assuming he has an infinite supply of coins.
