Construct an offline text editor that reads a given text and a sequence of editing commands and reports the resultant text after performing the commands. The editor includes a text buffer and a cursor, with commands to manipulate the cursor and text. The specific commands include moving the cursor forward or backward by character or word, inserting text, and deleting characters or words. The text contains letters, digits, and certain punctuation, and has a maximum length of 1,000 characters with up to 100 characters for inserted text.
