The problem involves calculating the time it takes for a student named sirokurostone to travel from their starting station to A津大学 using the Seishun 18 ticket, while also picking up a friend who shares the same ticket type. Given the delay information and distances between various stations, the task is to find the minimum travel time from the starting station to the friend's station and then from there to the destination station, where A津大学 is located. The input consists of multiple datasets, each specifying the number of stations, railway connections, and their respective distances and delays, with the output being the total travel time in hours.
