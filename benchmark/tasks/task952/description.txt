There are N barbecue restaurants along a street numbered from 1 to N from west to east, with the distance between each restaurant defined in an array A. Each restaurant offers meals of varying deliciousness in exchange for tickets numbered from 1 to M. Joisino has M tickets that can each be used only once, but any number of them can be used at a restaurant. Starting from any restaurant, Joisino wants to maximize her happiness calculated as the total deliciousness of the meals eaten minus the total distance traveled. The goal is to find her maximum possible happiness.
