A cheetah and a cheater are playing a game of Nim with N piles of stones, where each pile initially contains a_i stones. The cheetah plays first, and players alternate turns taking stones from the piles. Before the game starts, the cheater can take zero or one stone from each pile to ensure he can win regardless of the cheetah's moves. Your task is to compute the minimum number of stones the cheater has to eat to guarantee a win. If there is no way for the cheater to guarantee a win, print -1.
