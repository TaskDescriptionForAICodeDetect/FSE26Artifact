Takahashi has come to a party as a special guest with N ordinary guests. Each guest has a certain power represented by A_i. Takahashi intends to perform M handshakes to maximize the happiness at the party, which starts at 0. A handshake involves choosing one guest for his left hand and another guest for his right hand, simultaneously increasing the happiness by the sum of their powers A_x + A_y. No handshake can be repeated. The goal is to determine the maximum happiness after M handshakes.
