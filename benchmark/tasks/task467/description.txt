You want to compete in ICPC (Internet Contest of Point Collection). In this contest, you move around in N websites, numbered 1 to N, within a time limit and collect as many points as possible. You can start and end on any website. There are M links between the websites, and you can move using these links. In each website i, there is an advertisement, and you can get p_i points by watching it in t_i seconds. You can decide whether to watch the advertisement when you start or move into a website, but you cannot watch the same advertisement more than once before using any link. Moreover, you cannot watch the advertisement at website i more than k_i times. The goal is to compute the maximum points you can collect within T seconds.
