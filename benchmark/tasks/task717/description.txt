Takahashi is at an all-you-can-eat restaurant that offers N kinds of dishes. It takes A_i minutes to eat the i-th dish, and each dish has a deliciousness score B_i. The rules state that Takahashi can only order one dish at a time, cannot order the same dish more than once, and after T - 0.5 minutes from the first order, no new orders can be placed, although he can continue eating the dish he has ordered. The goal is to calculate the maximum happiness Takahashi can achieve, which is the sum of the deliciousness of the dishes he eats.
