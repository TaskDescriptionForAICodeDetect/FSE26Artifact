Dolphin is planning to generate a certain chemical substance C by preparing a solution that is a mixture of two substances A and B in the ratio of M_a:M_b. He needs to purchase packages of chemicals from a pharmacy, where each package contains specific grams of substances A and B and is sold for a certain price in yen. The challenge is to find the minimum amount of money required to generate substance C using all contents of the purchased packages. If it's not possible to generate substance C with any combination of packages, report that fact.
