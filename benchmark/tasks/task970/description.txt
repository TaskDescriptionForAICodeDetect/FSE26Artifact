There are N towns in the Takahashi Kingdom conveniently numbered from 1 to N. The king, Takahashi, plans to inspect these towns over M days, visiting town c_i on the i-th day. The tour starts from town 1 (the capital) and ends at town c_M. The king wants to pave one-way roads between towns as he travels. At the end of his tour, it should be possible to travel between any two towns using the newly paved roads. The task is to determine how many sequences of towns c satisfy this condition.
