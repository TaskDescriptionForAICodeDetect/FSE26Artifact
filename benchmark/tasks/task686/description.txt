You are going to eat X red apples and Y green apples. You have A red apples with deliciousness values p_1, p_2, ..., p_A, B green apples with deliciousness values q_1, q_2, ..., q_B, and C colorless apples with deliciousness values r_1, r_2, ..., r_C. Before eating a colorless apple, you can paint it red or green, which will categorize it accordingly. Your goal is to select apples in a way that maximizes the total deliciousness of the apples you eat, considering that you can optimally color zero or more colorless apples.
